ABOUT US

Friends in life, partners in photography.

We love to work together, especially when it’s about photography. That’s why we made a decision, and started a new project- Event photography with immediately printed pictures. Precise and fast work, beautiful results. Two enthusiastic photographers, one event, several high quality printed pictures, satisfied guests and lots of fun!

  • Family Events

Always looking for new opportunities!

Can’t wait to work together, and add some extra fun to the everyday-life.
